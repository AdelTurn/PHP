Menu

Dining is never just about the food, it is about who you share it with and the experience.

Alonzo's strives to create the ultimate dining experience by mixing together incredible food, atmosphere, and service. You will never be a customer at Alonzo's, but a and honored guest. We only serve the freshest ingredients from our fruits and vegetables to our meats. Everything is of the highest quality and locally sourced. All food cooked in the Alonzo’s kitchen is hand crafted by our chefs from scratch.



Alonzo's is the namesake of Alonzo Lucchese, the head chef and owner of Alonzo's.

Alonzo was different from the other children growing up. While the other kids loved to play outside, Alonzo preferred to stay inside and cook with his mother. As an immigrant from Naples, Italy she had tons to teach him about Italian cuisine. From a young age Alonzo knew his goal in life was to open a restaurant, but no just any restaurant. Alonzo dreamed of having the most spectacular restaurant one could imagine.





From cooking with mom, to the top of his class, and now
owning his own restaurant.

Alonzo continued his love for cooking all the way to culinary school where he was the star of his class. After culinary school Alonzo saved up some money by working at other fine dining establishments, and in 2016 he finally opened up Alonzo's. Ever since he and his team have been working to achieve his childhood goal.

Alonzo's

Alonzo’s is a fine dining Italian restaurant that has been serving it's community with great food and impeccable service for 5 years now and will continue to do so.
