Who is Magic Paws

The "Magic" Behind Magic Paws

We call ourselves Magic Paws not because we have some sort of special powers but because as corny as it might sound, we care. That is our secret, and we are not afraid to show it. We care about you and your furry friend so much that we do everything in our power to give you the best service imaginable. We are dedicated to having the best staff, open and honest communication, and giving your access to the most affordable care. We provide all the varieties of services from general health care checkups to emergency care. This is what sets us apart from the rest, this is why work everyday proud to be apart of you and your pets lives.

Sara Johnson

Head Veterinarian and Owner

Sara Johnson has always loved animals and always knew she wanted to become a veterinarian. When she was 18 she went of to school to study veterinary medicine, and became a Veterinarian. In 2008 she had her idea to open up a Veterinary Clinic and in 2011 she finally did with Magic Paws. Ever since then she has been getting better and better at her craft.

Alan Green

Veterinarian

Alan Green grew up with a single father who was a zoo keeper, and Alan loved going with his dad to take care of the animals. He spent all of his weekends and summer days helping his father and grew his love for animals. While he was in college he met Sara and she mentored him and told him about Magic Paws. When he finally graduated he joined up with Sara in 2013 to help run Magic Paws.

Jennifer Smith

Veterinarian

Jennifer Smith grew up in a hunting family and never really cared for animals, when she was 18 though she moved out and ended up getting her own dog and cat. This changed her perspective on animals and really started to care deeply about them. She ended up changing her major and joined Magic Paws in 2016. Ever since then she has been providing the highest quality care to all animals that walk through our door.

Magic Paws
