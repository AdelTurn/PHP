Welcome to Magic Paws Veterinary Clinic, we are so excited to welcome you to our family. Why Choose Magic Paws

Expert Staff

Magic paws only employs the best of the best. From our incredible front desk staff who makes everything on your part easy. All the way to our doctors who make sure to give your pets the best care possible.

Honesty

Health and health scares can be really tough. But with Magic Paws we make it a point to be open and honest with all of our guests. Rest assured that you will be kept up to date with everything happening with your best friend.

Communication

Open communication with all of our vets is always available to you. From regular check up updates to updates on surgeries or overnight visits. You will always be up to date.

Caring

All of our veterinarians are avid animal lovers. This means they really care about what happens to your little friend. This leads us to giving the best care to all the animals that come into our doors.
