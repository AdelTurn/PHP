OUR STORY

Henry Smith started Great Ape Care (GAC) on February 16th 2000 to help conserve the critically endangered apes of the world. Henry was interested in conservation ever since he was a little boy and as he grew he met other like minded individuals who are here to help him. The start of GAC was slow but through hard work GAC grew to become one of the biggest conservational efforts for great apes.

Our Mission is simple, it is to bring great ape populations back to healthy conditions. We do this through helping prevent deforestation and caring for injured or displaced great apes. The help and donations we get from people are what make sure we can achieve our mission. We still have much more to go, but through our efforts we have made great progress towards positive change. If you would like to join us check out our Contact page or Send a Donation our way. You can also check us out on most major social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter. Thank you so much for taking the time to learn more about us!
